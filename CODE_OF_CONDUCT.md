# 行为准则 | Code of Conduct

## 我们的承诺 | Our Pledge

作为本项目的贡献者和维护者，为了营造一个开放、友好的学习与协作环境，我们承诺：无论参与者的年龄、体型、残障状况、民族、性别认同与表达、经验水平、国籍、外貌、种族、宗教信仰或性取向如何，均不受骚扰和歧视。

We as members, contributors, and maintainers pledge to make participation in our project and community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

---

## 我们的标准 | Our Standards

### ✅ 有助于营造积极环境的行为 | Positive Behaviors

- 🤝 使用友善和包容的语言 | Using welcoming and inclusive language
- 💡 尊重不同的观点和经验 | Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- 🙏 虚心接受建设性的批评和建议 | Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- 🎯 以社区和项目的最大利益为重 | Focusing on what is best for the community
- ❤️ 对其他社区成员表示理解和关心 | Showing empathy towards other community members
- 📚 鼓励初学者提问和参与 | Encouraging beginners to ask questions and participate

### ❌ 不可接受的行为 | Unacceptable Behaviors

- 🚫 使用带有性暗示的语言或图像，以及不受欢迎的性关注或骚扰 | The use of sexualized language or imagery and unwelcome sexual attention or advances
- 🚫 恶意挑衅、侮辱性/贬损性评论，以及人身攻击或政治攻击 | Trolling, insulting/derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks
- 🚫 公开或私下的骚扰行为 | Public or private harassment
- 🚫 未经明确许可发布他人的个人信息（如住址、电子邮箱等） | Publishing others' private information without explicit permission
- 🚫 其他在专业环境中被合理认定为不当的行为 | Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

---

## 我们的责任 | Our Responsibilities

项目维护者有责任为「可接受的行为」标准做出诠释，并针对任何不可接受的行为采取适当且公正的纠正措施。

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying the standards of acceptable behavior and are expected to take appropriate and fair corrective action in response to any instances of unacceptable behavior.

项目维护者有权且有责任删除、编辑或拒绝与本行为准则不一致的评论、提交、代码、Issue、以及其他贡献内容，并可临时或永久封禁任何做出不当、威胁、冒犯或有害行为的贡献者。

Project maintainers have the right and responsibility to remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and other contributions that are not aligned to this Code of Conduct, or to ban temporarily or permanently any contributor for other behaviors that they deem inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful.

---

## 适用范围 | Scope

本行为准则适用于所有项目空间（包括但不限于 Issue、Pull Request、讨论区等），也适用于个人在公共场合代表本项目或社区时的行为。

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces (including but not limited to Issues, Pull Requests, Discussions), and also applies when an individual is representing the project or its community in public spaces.

---

## 执行 | Enforcement

如遇到辱骂、骚扰或其他不可接受的行为，请通过以下**私密方式**向项目维护者报告（请勿在公开 Issue 中举报，以保护举报者隐私）：

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainers **privately** (please do not use public Issues, to protect reporter privacy):

- 🔒 通过 [GitHub Security Advisories](../../security/advisories/new) 提交私密报告 | Submit a private report via [GitHub Security Advisories](../../security/advisories/new)

所有投诉都将被审查和调查，并以适当的方式进行回应。项目维护者有义务对事件报告者的信息保密。

All complaints will be reviewed and investigated and will result in a response that is deemed necessary and appropriate to the circumstances. The project team is obligated to maintain confidentiality with regard to the reporter of an incident.
